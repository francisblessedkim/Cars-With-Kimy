Introduction: Story Being Told by My Site

Cars with Kimy tells the story of the ultimate newsletter for car lovers and enthusiasts. Cars with Kimy mainly aims to provide exclusive insights, beautiful visuals, and next-level updates from the world of automobiles. The story is based on delivering the latest industrial news with expert reviews and giving a unique experience to automotive enthusiasts.

Structure of the Site

The Homepage’s main content is divided into two sections which are the main-container-left and main-container-right. Left displays top stories with details and right displays trending stories with details.

The Head Section, Header and Footer of all the webpages in the site share the same structure with the head section containing metadata, links and the stylesheets. The header contains the title, navigation menu and search bar while the footer contains information about the newsletter, newsletter subscription form, social media icons and an audio player with copyright information at the very bottom.

Inspiration for the Site

  1. The main inspiration for this site was mainly due to my passion for cars. Personally, I’m a car enthusiast and I genuinely have a deep love for automobiles whether it’s classic cars, sports cars, or even electric cars or any other category. This was the strong reason behind the creation of the Cars with Kimy Newsletter.
  2. Expertise and knowledge: my expertise and knowledge in the automotive industry have played an essential role that I gained from watching and following other car enthusiasts such as Supercar Blondie, carwow and reading other blogs like autoblog and because of that I wanted to showcase my knowledge and provide valuable information to the public.
  3. Stay informed: another factor in creating cars with Kimy was also just to make sure I stay informed with the latest trends and developments in the automotive industry.

Accessibility

  • Responsive Web Design: The site is designed to be responsive, adapting to various screen sizes and devices. Whether you're browsing on a computer, tablet, or smartphone, you'll have an optimal viewing experience.
  • Alt Text for Images: Images on the website are accompanied by descriptive alt text. This makes the content accessible to users with disabilities.

Usability

  1. Clear Navigation: The website has a straightforward and easy-to-navigate menu in the header. Users can quickly find their way to different sections such as "Home," "About Us," "Current News," "Top Cars," and "Contact Us."
  2. Responsive Design: The website is designed to be responsive, ensuring that it adapts to various screen sizes and devices. This is essential for providing a consistent user experience across desktop and mobile devices.
  3. Engaging Content: The website features engaging content, including articles with images and descriptive text. This can keep users interested and encourage them to explore further.
